This is My Education Journey



TASKI UMMU AIMAN

My educational journey started from Taski Ummu Aiman located in Beseri, Perlis. I started my education when I was 4 years old in 2006 and graduated from Taski Umm Aiman in 2008 when I was 6 years old.


SEKOLAH RENDAH AGAMA AT-TARBIYYAH AL-ISLAMIYYAH

I continued my education at Sekolah Rendah Agama At-Tarbiyyah Al-Islamiyyah (SRAATI) located in Beseri, Perlis. I continued my education at SRAATI at the age of 7 in 2009 and finished my education at this primary school in 2014 when I was 12 years old. I took two tests when I was 12 years old, UPSR and PRAI. My Primary School Assessment (UPSR) results were 2A and 3B and the Primary Islamic Religious Assessment (PRAI) with 3A and 3B.


SEKOLAH MENENGAH KEBANGSAAN TENGKU SULEIMAN

I continued my education to secondary school which is Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Tengku Suleiman which is located in Beseri, Perlis. I started high school education in 2015 when I was 13 years old. In 2017, I sat for the PT3 exam and got quite satisfactory results and sat for another test which is SPM in 2019. The result for SPM was also quite satisfactory and continued my studies at the University level.


UNIVERSITI TEKNOLOGI MARA CAWANGAN KEDAH

I continued my studies at the university level in 2020 when I was 18 years old. I learned a lot when I was at university.
